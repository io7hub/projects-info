El Salvador SME DX Strategy

A Lightweight, Modular, and Action-Oriented Pipeline for Digital Transformation

The Challenge: High Barriers to DX

Traditional MES/ERP solutions present significant risks for SMEs due to high initial investment, complex implementation, and heavy maintenance costs, hindering data-driven growth.

The Vision: Lightweight Approach

We propose a modular, low-cost IoT platform that enables rapid, targeted implementation. This strategy minimizes investment risk while delivering tangible results quickly, fostering a sustainable DX.

Data-Driven Diagnosis: Identifying the Gaps

An analysis of El Salvadoran SMEs reveals a critical gap. While capabilities in Production and Quality are strong, core operational areas like resource management, market responsiveness, and sustainability are significantly underdeveloped, scoring just 2.0 out of 5.

The Solution: A 4-Step Closed-Loop Pipeline

1

Data & Analysis

IoT data is collected and processed in real-time, then visualized in a Streamlit dashboard for analysis.

2

Alert & Discussion

When KPIs deviate, Streamlit automatically sends a webhook alert to a dedicated Google Chat.

3

Action & Tracking

Based on the discussion, a formal task is created in Trello, assigning responsibility and a deadline.

4

Closed-Loop

This process ensures that data insights are converted into concrete actions, Optimizing.

The Core Engine: Patner E's 4 Key Modules

Fabrik Monitor

Real-time OEE and equipment status monitoring to maximize production efficiency.

QSI Keeper

Automated data logging for Critical Control Points (CCP) to ensure quality and compliance.

Eco-Sense

Utility consumption analysis to identify waste and reduce energy costs, targeting sustainability goals.

MES Lite

A lightweight manufacturing execution system for streamlined work order and inventory tracking.

Tailored Implementation: Company-Specific Roadmaps

Partner A

Maturity: Very High (83)

Challenge: Requires advanced solutions for energy efficiency and deeper process integration.

Solution: Implement `Fabrik Monitor` and `Eco-Sense` to optimize OEE and reduce energy consumption.

Partner M

Maturity: High / Upper-Medium

Challenge: Lack of data standardization and fragmented ERP integration.

Solution: Deploy `QSI Keeper` for automated quality logging and `MES Lite` to standardize data flow to ERP.

Partner T

Maturity: Medium

Challenge: Manual reporting processes and limited data analysis capabilities.

Solution: Integrate `MES Lite` for performance tracking and visualize data with `Fabrik Monitor` dashboards.

Ensuring Success: Overcoming Potential Challenges

Data Standardization

ERDE experts will lead initial PoCs to establish clear data collection rules and formats, ensuring data quality from the start.

Long-Term Scalability

A clear roadmap for upgrading from `MES Lite` to full MES or integrating with third-party systems will be provided for advanced companies like Partner A.

Personnel Training

Comprehensive, hands-on training programs will be conducted to empower managers to interpret data and utilize collaboration tools effectively.
