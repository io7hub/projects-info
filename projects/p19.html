DX Strategy for LATAM SMEs

An Action-Oriented, Lightweight IoT Pipeline for Rapid KPI Improvement

The Starting Point: Data-Driven Diagnosis

Our analysis shows that while partners excel in production and quality (Scoring 5.0/5.0), they face urgent challenges in sustainability and market responsiveness, which are critical for future growth. These gaps are our primary targets for DX intervention.

The Technology Blueprint: Lightweight IoT Architecture

1. Edge

Sensors & a local IoT Gateway collect real-time data (power, temp, status) directly from machinery.

→
↓

2. Cloud

A lightweight backend processes and stores data, integrating with essential ERP/MES modules.

→
↓

3. Application

Web dashboards (Grafana/Power BI) provide real-time KPI visualization and decision support.

The Action Plan: Two High-Impact Pilot Tracks

Track A: Sustainability

Targeting the lowest KPI score of 2.0 in Sustainable Operations.

Challenge & Solution

Problem: High energy waste from CNC machines without real-time monitoring.

Solution: Deploy a lightweight Energy Management System (EMS) to collect and analyze power consumption data.

Expected KPI Improvement

Track B: Market Responsiveness

Addressing the critical KPI score of 2.0 in Market Responsiveness.

Challenge & Solution

Problem: Inaccurate delivery forecasts due to a disconnect between ERP orders and actual production progress.

Solution: Implement an MES-Lite module to sync order data with real-time process completion data.

Expected KPI Improvement

Roadmap to Success

1. Start with PoCs

Begin with targeted 3-month pilot projects on the most critical KPIs to deliver visible results and build DX momentum.

2. Leverage Local Partners

Collaborate with the local tech partner (IT Solution) for hardware installation and maintenance, ensuring cost-effective and rapid deployment.

3. Empower People

Provide hands-on training for managers to use the data dashboards, enabling them to identify inefficiencies and make informed decisions.
